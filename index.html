About Bert Games

One Dev. Big Dreams.

Hi! I'm Bertus Engelbrecht, a solo indie game developer with a passion for creating fun, creative and memorable gaming experiences across every platform.

From massive open-world survival RPGs on Steam, to casual clickers on mobile, Roblox adventures, and handy web tools — Bert Games is where imagination meets code. Every game is a labour of love, built from scratch by one person who genuinely loves games.

The mission is simple: make games that are fun first, and let the community help shape what comes next.
