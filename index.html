About Bert Games

One Developer.
Unlimited Ideas.

Bert Games is the solo indie studio of Bertus Engelbrecht — a developer with a genuine passion for creating fun, imaginative gaming experiences across every platform.

From massive open-world survival RPGs on Steam to mobile clickers, Roblox worlds and interactive web tools, each project is built from the ground up by one person who truly loves games.

The goal is simple: build games that are fun first, and let the community help shape what comes next.
